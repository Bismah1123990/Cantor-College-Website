College Services & Information

Latest News

Cantor College recently hosted the Software Engineering Student of the Year Awards. Short-listed candidates from across the country attended this prestigious one-day event, celebrating excellence and innovation in the field of software engineering.

Academic Registry

The Academic Registry is responsible for academic regulations, assessments, awards, student records, and course validation. They ensure that all academic processes are managed efficiently and fairly.

Catering Services

Catering Services provide both on-site and off-site catering solutions for students, staff, and events. They ensure high-quality food and service across the College.

Financial Services

Located on the second floor, the Financial Services team manages all aspects of student finance as well as the College’s budgeting and financial planning.

Information Systems Services (ISS)

The ISS team delivers and maintains the College’s computing infrastructure, including hardware and software systems. They also operate the staff helpdesk for technical support.

Marketing Services

The Marketing Team supports the promotion of College events and new courses. They also assist with press releases and external communications.

Personnel Services

Personnel Services handle all staff-related enquiries, including pay, contracts, and working conditions. The team is based on the third floor.

Facilities Management

The Facilities team is responsible for the general care and maintenance of the College. All enquiries should be directed through the main helpdesk.
