Library & Learning Resources

Access to Resources

All students and staff at Cantor College are entitled to register as members of the Library and Resource Centre. These facilities support both academic study and professional development throughout your time at the College.

Membership may also be extended to external individuals or organisations for a fee. Access for non-members can be arranged for study purposes where appropriate.

Facilities & Services

  • Extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources
  • Dedicated study areas and computer workstations
  • Access to online databases and research materials
  • Support for independent and group study

Rules and Responsibilities

Use of the Library and Resource Centre requires adherence to College rules designed to ensure a safe, fair, and productive environment for all users.

These rules apply across all College sites, including libraries and workstation rooms. Staff are authorised to enforce these regulations where necessary.

Disciplinary Procedures

Failure to follow the rules may result in disciplinary action, including restricted access to facilities. In serious cases, College regulations for students or staff procedures may be applied.
