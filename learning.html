Library & Study Spaces

Our modern library offers a comprehensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources. Quiet study areas and group workspaces are available to support different learning styles.

Computing Facilities

Students have access to state-of-the-art computer labs equipped with the latest software for programming, design, and data analysis. High-speed internet and specialist tools support coursework and project development.

Online Learning Platforms

Cantor College provides access to virtual learning environments where students can access lecture materials, submit assignments, and collaborate with peers and tutors online.

Student Support

Additional support is available through academic advisors, workshops, and one-to-one tutoring sessions to help students achieve their full potential.
