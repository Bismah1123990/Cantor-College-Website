Our Facilities

Cantor College is located in a modern and attractively refurbished building, designed to support both teaching and student life. The campus includes dedicated computing laboratories, lecture theatres, tutorial rooms, and comfortable student workspaces.

The building spans over 9,500m², accommodates more than 240 staff, and provides teaching facilities for over 1,600 students. Additional amenities include on-site catering and convenient parking facilities.

Key Facilities

  • Campus-wide Wi-Fi access
  • Flexible teaching rooms (25–80 students)
  • Specialist faculty and IT facilities
  • Double-height lecture theatre
  • Three-storey glass atrium
  • Meeting and study rooms
  • Reception and administrative areas
  • Catering outlets
  • Accessible parking for disabled badge holders
  • Cycle storage facilities
  • On-site gallery space
