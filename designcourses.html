Our Design Courses

Cantor College is an internationally connected creative community bringing together a diverse range of disciplines under one roof. We aim to shape our students' futures, preparing them to make a meaningful impact through applied knowledge and creativity.

Our art and design courses go beyond traditional teaching by encouraging alternative ways of thinking, making, and communicating. Students are provided with the space, tools, and inspiration needed to develop their creative practice and build a clear career path.

You will benefit from expert teaching delivered by active practitioners and researchers who promote innovative and resourceful approaches to problem-solving. These experiences help students both recognise and create opportunities for better outcomes.

Throughout your studies, you will collaborate with peers in well-equipped studios, workshops, and digital environments. At the same time, you will gain professional experience by working on real-world projects through our links with commercial clients, cultural institutions, and organisations.
